How to Use OneClickInstall

1

Browse & Search: Use the category filter on the left or the search bar on top to find your desired applications.

2

Select Your Apps: Click on the application cards to add them to your installation queue. Selected apps will show a green border.

3

Review Selection: Check the counter at the bottom sticky bar to confirm the total number of applications selected.

4

Initiate Download: Click the large green **INSTALL SELECTED** button to start the bundled download process.

5

Confirmation SMS/Email: Once the download is complete, a confirmation notification (simulated SMS/Email) will be triggered.
